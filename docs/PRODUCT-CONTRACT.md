# Product contract

## The job

Help someone decide whether the visible copy on one real slide can remain one page, needs editing, or has earned a split.

## Inputs the tool may use

- how the page is encountered: presented live, sent to read, or both;
- the exact visible copy;
- optional expert choices about page job, audience, evidence, timing, sources, and layout pressure;
- the browser-rendered dimensions and loaded type.

## Outputs

- one primary verdict: keep one, edit, or split;
- measured copy and block counts;
- a real 16:9 preview and physical-fit status;
- plain reasons, limits, and next moves;
- suggested split boundaries only where a mechanical boundary exists.

## Boundaries

Slide Check does not read intent from arbitrary prose. It does not judge truth, originality, taste, persuasion, brand fit, or whether a project deserves to exist. Reading-time bands are editorial starting points, not universal laws. A split suggestion still needs a person to judge coherence.

## Privacy

Working text and state remain in the browser. The production tool has no account, database, analytics, upload, or runtime AI call.
